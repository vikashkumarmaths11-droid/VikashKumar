<h2>Research on Fermat's Last Theorem</h2>
<p>
My research focuses on geometric approaches to Fermat's Last Theorem,
one of the most celebrated problems in number theory. The work explores
triangle-based interpretations, geometric structures, and elementary
methods for analysing higher-power Diophantine equations.
</p>
<p>
The objective is to investigate relationships between geometry and
number theory, providing new perspectives on equations of the form
xⁿ + yⁿ = zⁿ for n > 2.
</p>
